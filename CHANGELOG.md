# Changelog

## 2.0.0 - 2026-07-20

- Reposition Vocaby as a combined word and expression learning app with five primary tabs.
- Add two-sided 3D study cards with immediate SM-2 scheduling and three-direction swipe grading.
- Expand practice with configurable choice, spelling, listening, and mixed quizzes, timed feedback, and retry flows.
- Add seven- and thirty-day trends, a fifteen-week heatmap, learning-state distribution, and an achievement wall.
- Introduce persistent achievements, confetti celebrations, the sky-to-teal brand system, richer motion, and haptic feedback.
- Add configurable 10–100 daily goals, pronunciation autoplay, appearance controls, and two-step progress reset.

## 1.1.0 - 2026-07-20

- Expand the bundled reviewed vocabulary bank to 18,603 unique entries.
- Include review-approved pronunciations, bilingual meanings, examples, and source attribution.
- Preserve vocabulary counts by basic, intermediate, and advanced learning levels.

## 1.0.0 - 2026-07-15

- Establish the first stable Vocaby public version.
- Provide native daily practice, review, library, notifications, and widgets.
- Refine every learning screen around Apple Music and Apple HIG conventions.
- Redesign vocabulary cards and quizzes for Traditional Chinese learners of English.
